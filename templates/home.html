Predict Breast Cancer

Predict Breast Cancer is an online tool that helps medical professions detect early stages of breast cancer via mammography.

Start Predict

Who

Predict breast cancer is for medical professionals only. Please contact us to sign up for an account.

What

We ask for some details and a mammography of the patient. It then uses data about similiar cases to detect whether or not if the patient has cancer or not.

How

We use deep learning techniques, specifically CNN model.
